About YISA

The Youth in Sustainable Aquaculture (YISA) Program, a partnership with the Mastercard Foundation aims to enhance resilient livelihoods through innovative aquaculture practices.

The project aims to change perceptions of the potential offered by the aquaculture sector for entrepreneurship and employment, building knowledge and capacity so that young people, especially women, are attracted by the opportunities and growth potential offered by working in aquaculture. It will also address socio-cultural norms and attitudes to increase the inclusion of women in a sector that is currently male-dominated.

Program Details

YISA is designed to create sustainable opportunities for young people in aquaculture

Location

Kisumu, Homa Bay, Siaya, Kakamega, Busia, and Kilifi

Eligibility

Ages 23-32 years from the six counties to join the YISA program

Goal

Create 150,000 jobs in aquaculture for young people
