Explore Aquarech

Powering Growth in Aquaculture

Aquarech is Kenya's first fish farming platform that enables fish farmers, fish feed manufacturers, and fish traders to trade and build trusting long-lasting partnerships.

Who We Are

If you haven't heard of us yet, we guarantee you won't be able to forget us once you do.

Our team is comprised of only the best employees in the industry, all of whom work tirelessly to achieve the objectives they have set for themselves.

Our Vision

To transform African aquaculture into a sustainable, tech-driven industry where smallholder farmers thrive as commercial entrepreneurs, eradicating poverty and hunger through inclusive finance and climate-smart solutions.

Our Mission

We empower smallholder fish farmers with collateral-free loans, high-quality inputs, and guaranteed markets through our integrated AquaFedha platform. By combining blended finance, mobile technology, and aquaculture expertise, we bridge the gap between subsistence farming and profitable commercial production.
