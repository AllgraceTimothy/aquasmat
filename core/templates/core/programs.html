Our Programs

Welcome to our programs designed to transform the future of aquaculture. We offer initiatives such as the Youth in Sustainable Aquaculture and the Aquaculture Livelihood Improvement Program, which help small and medium-sized fish farmers thrive.

Explore Our Programs

Our mission focuses on empowering women and youth to engage in fish production, ensuring a profitable and sustainable future for all. Join us in making a difference in the aquaculture sector!

Youth In Sustainable Aquaculture

YISA supports young people, especially young women, in creating dignified and fulfilling work opportunities in the aquaculture and mariculture sectors.

Learn More

Aquaculture Livelihood Improvement Program

ALIP empowers women in the fish production industry by supporting small and medium-sized fish farms. Through innovative training and resources, we help them transition into profitable ventures, creating opportunities and fostering growth in the community.
